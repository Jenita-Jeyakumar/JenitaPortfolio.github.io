Graduates Placement Analysis using R

Analysing how likely it is for MBA graduates to get placed
and factors that influence their success.

About the data

The data set used for this project is from Kaggle.com. It contains information about the placement status of graduates from an MBA course. The variables include exam results, board and stream of secondary school, higher secondary school, field of degree and MBA specialization, of male and female students. The data also contains Employability test scores and work experience status for all students and the salary of the placed students.

Full Story

As part of the main analysis of this data set, we will look into factors that determine whether or not a student gets placed.
