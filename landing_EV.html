Electric Vehicle Rebate Analysis

Analysing the success of rebate program in motivating buyers to choose fuel-efficient alternatives
to help achieve net-zero carbon emissions.

About the data

The data set obtained from data.world.com represents the initiative by New York State Energy Research and Development Authority (NYSERDA), that offers a rebate for the purchase of new Electric Vehicles (EVs). The data consists of car manufacturers who are part of the program, and the rebate they offer depending on the vehicle model and EV type. It also contains the reductions in CO2 emissions and petroleum usage annually that is attributed to each vehicle make.

Full Story

We see through the analysis, how likley buyers are to opt for greener alternatives that reduce their reliance on fossil fuels, decrease the negative impacts on the environment while saving money with the rebate offer.

Through this project we study if the rebate program in New York has been a success, the factors that influence the results, and whether other State governments can learn something valuable from New York's endeavor to achieve a win for the environment.
