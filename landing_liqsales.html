Liquor Sales Analysis using Python and Tableau

Analysing one of the fastest growing markets in North America, that generates billions in revenue.
A deep dive into what it takes to be the jack of this trade.

About the data

The dataset obtained from Kaggle, was originally obtained from an open data source published by the State of Iowa. It gives details of liquor sales in the state of Iowa in US, such as the amount and price of liquor sold, time of purchase, the type of liquor, vendor, store location, and so on.

Full Story

The data is analyzed based on various factors, to draw insights into the liquor market. The analysis would include studying which type of liquor and vendors are generating more revenue. The time of purchase can show the trend in the market across the year, and the liquor category in which the vendors are doing good can be analyzed.
